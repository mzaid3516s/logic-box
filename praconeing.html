Definition of Health ?

Health is a state of Physical, Mental, and social well-being (not merely the absence of disease or infirmity ).

Importance of Health

Types of Health

Physical and mental health are two essential components of overall well-being.

Mental Health

On the other hand, mental health focuses on emotional and psychological well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and respond to stress, challenges, and relationships. Good mental health allows individuals to handle emotions, cope with stress, and make sound decisions. Both physical and mental health are interconnected, and balancing them is key to leading a healthy, fulfilling life.

Physical Health

Physical health refers to the condition of the body and its ability to perform daily activities efficiently. It involves proper nutrition, regular exercise, adequate sleep, and the absence of disease or injury. Maintaining good physical health helps improve energy levels and longevity.

Physical Health

Physical health refers to the well-being of the body and its ability to perform daily activities without discomfort, illness, or injury. It encompasses a range of factors, including proper nutrition, regular exercise, adequate rest, and the body's ability to function efficiently. Maintaining good physical health helps prevent chronic diseases, boosts energy levels, and promotes overall well-being.
